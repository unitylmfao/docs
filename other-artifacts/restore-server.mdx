---
name: "How to restore a server"
description: "How to restore a server from a backup"
icon: "clock-rotate-left"
---

<Note>
    If you have the <strong>Healer</strong> package, you have access to `Instant
    Restores`
</Note>

<Steps>
    <Step title="Edit Server Data">
        Head to `Dashboard ➔ Manage Servers ➔ Your Server` <br /> and edit the{" "}
        <strong>Server ID & Role ID</strong> data.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Start the restore">
        Head to `Dashboard ➔ Manage Servers ➔ Your Server ➔ Backups` <br /> and
        choose the backup you wish to restore!
    </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
    Depending on your plan and how large the backup was, it may take a while to
    restore your server.
</Tip>
